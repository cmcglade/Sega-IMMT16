Here you will be able to find out what I am thinking at the moment, what jobs I have developed and how to get in touch. In order to "break the ice" a little, let me introduce myself...

My first experience was as a Web Design/Development and e-Learning intern. As soon as I graduated, I was given the opportunity to work at IBM as a Web Developer, where I stayed for 7 years.

At the very end of 2013, however, I was given the challenge to integrate a start-up, being responsible not only for the e-commerce platform development project, but also the company's digital marketing strategy, customer care and many other activities which encompass a startup's daily activities.

My most recent job experience was at Accenture, from May/2015 to September/2016, where I worked with User Experience and Responsive Interface Design for web and mobile applications.

In my spare time, I enjoy cooking. I love learning new techniques and creating new recipes. My dream is to become the next MasterChef!
