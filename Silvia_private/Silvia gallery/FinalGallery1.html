Lets Explore!

The experience of new culture, places, and people is what makes me happy. I love travelling and capturing beautiful moments that will last me a life time. Enjoy the photos!

Venice, Italy

Venice, the capital of northern Italy’s Veneto region, is built on more than 100 small islands in a lagoon in the Adriatic Sea.

Dublin, Ireland

Dublin, capital of the Republic of Ireland, is on Ireland’s east coast at the mouth of the River Liffey.

Edinburgh, Scotland

Edinburgh is Scotland's compact, hilly capital. It has a medieval Old Town and elegant Georgian New Town with gardens and neoclassical buildings.

Vlore, Albania

Vlorë is a coastal city and municipality in southern Albania. It is the second largest port city of Albania.

Helsingor, Denmark

Helsingor, also known as Elsinore, is a port city in eastern Denmark. Overlooking the Oresund strait, the 15th-century Kronborg Castle provided the setting for Shakespeare’s “Hamlet.

London, England

London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom, is a 21st-century city with history stretching back to Roman times.
